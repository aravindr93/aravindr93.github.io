Short Bio

I am a PhD student at UW working on machine learning with Sham Kakade and Emo Todorov. I also collaborate with Sergey Levine and his students. For the summer of 2017, I interned at OpenAI with John Schulman and Igor Mordatch. We worked on improving policy gradient methods and applications in dexterous hand manipulation and multi-agent systems.

Previously, I was a student at IIT Madras with Balaraman Ravindran, and received the best undergraduate thesis award. Before switching focus to AI, I worked on statistical physics of complex networks and general optimization questions
